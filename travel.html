Portugal

My family and I love Portugal. It is a beautiful country with many gorgeous beaches, lovely food and very friendly people.

We have gone on holiday to Portugal for the past 10 years, usually to the lovely town of Alvor, which is full of Irish people and Irish pubs.

After a few years in Portugal we fell in love with it and haven't even considered going anywhere else since!

I hope to go there someday with my own family.
